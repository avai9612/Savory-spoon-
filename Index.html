Creamy Garlic Parmesan Pasta

20 mins
4 servings
Easy

Description

This creamy garlic parmesan pasta is the ultimate comfort food that comes together in just 20 minutes! The sauce is rich, creamy, and packed with garlic flavor, while the parmesan adds a salty, nutty depth. It's perfect for busy weeknights when you want something delicious without spending hours in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fettuccine or linguine pasta
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
